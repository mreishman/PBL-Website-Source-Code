About Us

Zeta Alpha Lambda is the local Chapter at Southeast Missouri State University of Phi Beta Lambda. Future Business Leaders of America – Phi Beta Lambda, FBLA - PBL, is the premier student organization for students interested in business, business education, and business related fields.

Mission

  • Our mission is to bring business and education together in a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career development programs.

  • This organization provides members with the ability to:

    • Develop leadership skills
    • Prepare for their career
    • Connect with business leaders
    • Learn vital communication skills
    • Compete in over 55 events
    • Network with members from across the nation
    • Connect with rewarding community service
