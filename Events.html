Competitve Events

As a professional Business Organization, Phi Beta Lambda travels to compete in a variety of professional competitve events. These include Accounting Principles, Computer Applications, Website Design, Marketing Concepts, and many more.


Competitve Events have three categories: individual, team, and chapter. Individual and team events focus on business skills and career development, while chapter events recognize individual chapter achievements and growth.
