Meeting Notes

SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes November 4, 2015

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, November 4, 2015, 7:17 p.m. President Cory Herzog called the meeting to order with Secretary Dylan Kennedy recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Dylan Kennedy via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported a chapter balance as of November 4, 2015, of $5,460.31. The report was filed.

Unfinished Business

None

New Business

President Cory Herzog stated members attending NFLC needs to stay for a quick meeting afterwards.

Secretary Dylan Kennedy reported that Thursday, November 12, 2015, at 6:15 p.m. at the Isle Casino will be the Chef’s Auction. Proceeds go to the March of Dimes. A maximum of 15 members is needed to help out with running items to bidders at the auction. Members that volunteered include Tyreek Williams, Gavin Ashley, McKenna Medlin, Maxim Van Deursen, Cory Herzog, Dylan Ashley, Kiana Samuel, Rebecca Ott, Misaki Makino and Dylan Kennedy. Members interested will meet at the Dempster parking lot at 5:45 p.m. dressed in a white dress shirt and black dress pants

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported that the NFLC remaining budget of $120.00 must be paid as soon as possible.

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported that homecoming shirts are $12.00. Members who have not paid for their shirt need to pay as soon as possible.

Membership chair Tyreek Williams announced a Thanksgiving night on Tuesday, November 17, 2015. Members need to meet at 5:30 p.m. in the Dempster parking lot to head to Lambert’s Café in Sikeston, MO.

Membership chair Tyreek Williams announced a social night on December 1, 2015, in St. Louis at a Blues hockey game.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher reported that the Beef O’Brady’s fundraiser on Saturday, October 31, 2015, from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. went well.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher reported a Wings Ect. fundraising night on Tuesday, November 10, 2015. All proceeds will go towards conferences and individual accounts. Flyers need to be distributed as soon as possible.

President Cory Herzog announced the March of Dimes mini walk on Saturday, November 14, 2015. It will be from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Members should meet in the Dempster parking lot at 9:15 a.m. If you cannot make it for the entire walk, that is okay. Please go to as much as you can.

President Cory Herzog reported highway clean up on Sunday, November 15, 2015, at 1:00 p.m.

Announcements

Chapter shirts are in. If members have paid their dues then they must see President Cory Herzog for their free t-shirt.

Next chapter meeting will be Wednesday, November 18, 2015, in Dempster room 247 at 7:00 p.m.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:29 p.m.

Dylan Kennedy, Secretary
