SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
September 10, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, September 10, 2014 7:03 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $3,499.87.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner discussed the Show Me Conference that takes place on October 12th. Registration fees of $40 will be due on September 24, 2014.

President Amber Joiner reported that we will be accepting nominees for the Presidential Search and Screening Committee.

President Amber Joiner reported that a Parliamentary Procedure PowerPoint would be used during this chapter meeting.

Vice President’s Report

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that Homecoming will take place on October 4th and that the chapter will be partnering with SAI.

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that a volunteer is needed for Homecoming Field Day that will take place on Monday, September 29th in the event of tug-of-war.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that dues of $50 for the year or $30 for the semester are due on September 24, 2014.

Reporter’s Report

Reporter Matt Reishman reported that T-shirt designs will be voted on.

Reporter Matt Reishman informed members about the chapter’s Facebook page and website.

Community Service Report

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that we will have a highway cleanup service project at least once a semester

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that we will be baking dog treats during the semester.

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that we will be collecting used books to give to a book drive.

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that we will possibly be offering tutoring services this semester.

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that November is National Prematurity Awareness Month for the March of Dimes

Community service chairperson Bijan Tahghighi suggested members to email him if they have any ideas for community service projects.

Finance Committee Report

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported on the results of the car wash that took place on Saturday, September 6, 2014.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that there will be a Beef O’Brady’s pancake breakfast fundraiser.

Membership Committee Report

There was no membership committee report.

Unfinished Business

There was no unfinished business.

New Business

Bijan Tahghighi moved to allocate funds of $65.55 to Malli Tahghighi for NLC gas receipts.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Bijan Tahghighi moved to allocate funds of $57.30 to Josh James for NLC gas receipts.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Bethany Joiner moved to vote on the chapter t-shirt design.

With a vote of 4 to 6, the second design won and will be our chapter t-shirt design.

Bijan Tahghighi moved to have a pancake breakfast fundraiser at Beef O’Brady’s this Saturday.

Bethany Joiner moved to amend the motion to replace the word this with next.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Bijan Tahghighi moved to create the special committee titled Homecoming Committee with Emily Rybolt as the head and Bijan Tahghighi, Corey Herzog, and Tyler Rosemann as the three members.

Bethany Joiner moved to amend the motion by omitting Bijan Tahghighi, Corey Herzog, and Tyler Rosemann, and replacing them with Dylan Kennedy, Harman Malmi, and Ott.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tyler Rosemann moved to open the floor for nominations for a student to be considered to serve on the Presidential Search and Screening Committee.

Bijan Tahghighi nominated Tyler Rosemann, who accepted.

Brandon Oatsvall nominated Amber Joiner, who withdrew her nomination.

Tyler Rosemann will be the nominee to be considered to serve on the Presidential Search and Screening Committee.

Announcements

Our next chapter meeting will take place on September 24th.

Committee meetings will start on Wednesday, September 17th at 7:00 p.m.

Dues must be paid in full by September 24th. If there is some sort of conflict with this, please see either President Amber Joiner or Treasurer Corey Herzog.

If you want to attend the Show-Me Conference, the registration fees need to be paid with your dues. If there is a conflict let either President Amber Joiner or Treasurer Corey Herzog know.

Justin Bader announced that Chris Dzurick, a previous member of our chapter will be the new FBLA-PBL state advisor for Missouri.

Tyler Rosemann reported that all new members must see him following the adjournment of the meeting.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:43 p.m.
