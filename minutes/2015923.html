Meeting Notes

SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes September 23, 2015

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, September 23, 2015, 7:36 p.m. President Cory Herzog called the meeting to order with Secretary Dylan Kennedy recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Dylan Kennedy via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported a chapter balance as of September 23, 2015, of $4,065.34. The report was filed.

Unfinished Business

None

New Business

President Cory Herzog announced Field Day is coming up on October 12, 2015, at 7:00 p.m. with the Spirit Rally to take place right after at 8:00 p.m. Individuals who signed up for Field Day include:

Tug-of-War

Donut Challenge

Musical Chairs

Game of Life

  • Cassie Robertson

  • Sara Carney

  • Kiana Samuel

  • Brandon Szarek

  • Cass Wiseman

  • Ben Zimmer (maybe)

  • Matt Rolwing (maybe)

  • Tyreek Williams

  • Ben Zimmer

  • Dylan Kennedy

  • Valecious Andreius

  • Alona Ivchenko

  • Dazza Rutcheako

  • Maxim Van Deursen (maybe)

  • Brandon Szarek

***Two more participants are needed for the Donut Challenge

If you cannot make it to this, you must find a replacement by Friday, October 2, 2015.

Individuals signed up for the Spirit Rally include:

  • Dylan Kennedy

  • Joe Allison

  • Anna Richter

  • Kyle Amuleke

  • Matt Rolwing

  • Misaki Makino

***Two more individuals are able to participate in this dance. You MUST contact Secretary, Dylan Kennedy by Wednesday, September 30, 2015, if interested.

President Cory Herzog announced the National Fall Leadership Conference will be November 6-7, 2015, in Omaha, Nebraska. It is $60 for registration by October 9, 2015. Fundraisers will be going on in order to help pay for this trip.

Secretary Dylan Kennedy announced the Show-Me Leadership Conference at Six Flags will be October 10, 2015. The dues of $49 were due.

Treasurer Rebecca Ott announced to pay dues of $50/year or $30/semester to her.

Reporter Matt Reishman showed two designs for homecoming shirts. The designs were voted on and the shirts will be long-sleeve orange with a large hippo design on the back and a small front pocket design of PBL on the front.

Membership chair Tyreek Williams announced a tailgate at the football game on Saturday, September 26, 2015. Members are to meet at 10:30 am outside Houck Stadium.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher announced $199.20 was raised at the car wash that was partnered with Jimmy’s Friends. $50 of the proceeds will go to Jimmy’s Friends.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher announced a Beef O’Brady’s fundraiser will be on Saturday, October 31, 2015. Tickets of $6 each will be sold. Proceeds will go towards members’ goals for NFLC.

Community Service chair Melissa Stephens announced a Trick-or-Treat for cans will be Wednesday, October 28, 2015.

Community Service chair Melissa Stephens announced the March of Dimes mini-walk will be November 11, 2015 at Scott County Central high school.

A homecoming committee was formed. They will meet and start locking down parade ideas. Each PBL member will be required to work 2 hours a week on homecoming designs.

Dylan Kennedy moved to reimburse Cory Herzog $177.57 for the PBL handouts for recruitment. The motion was seconded and approved.

Rebecca Ott moved to reimburse Tyreek Williams $54.95 for candy during opening week events. The motion was seconded and approved.

Dylan Kennedy moved to reimburse Rebecca Ott $9.91 for photos for the informational board and treasurer expenses. The motion was seconded and approved.

Announcements

The next chapter meeting will be Wednesday, October 7, 2015.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 8:22 p.m.

Dylan Kennedy, Secretary
