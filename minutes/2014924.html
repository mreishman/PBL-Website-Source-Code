SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
September 24, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, September 24, 2014 7:03 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Tyler Rosemann via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that $68 was made from the car wash fundraiser.

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $3,734.32.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner reported that the Show Me Conference will be on October 18 starts at 9 am. Registration of $40 is due tonight. If you cannot pay tonight please let Cory or Amber know.

President Amber Joiner reported that NFLC voting will be taken during new business. NFLC registration is $60, charter bus fee of $200, and hotel fee of $40. The total cost of the trip will be $300 without funding. Amber stated that the goal of the chapter is to get enough funds so each member will only have to pay $100 total for the NFLC conference.

President Amber Joiner reported that Homecoming t-shirts have been ordered and $15 is to be brought to the Homecoming Spirit Rally to get your t-shirt.

Vice President’s Report

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that Homecoming yard art is turned in and can be seen at the UC.

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that we need 5 people to sign up for trivia this Friday at 7 pm at the North Recreation Center.

Vice President Emily Rybolt announced that Homecoming Field Day is on Monday at Houck Field House. The Homecoming Spirit Rally starts at 8 pm.

Vice President Emily Rybolt announced that the Homecoming Talent show is next Tuesday at 7 pm at the Show Me Center

Vice President Emily Rybolt announced that we will be working on the Homecoming float this weekend starting Friday afternoon at 3 pm. Should you need to contact her, check the official minutes sent via email Members will be making tissue paper flowers, painting the backboard, and pomping chicken wire cacti.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that dues are due tonight $50 for whole year or $30 for the semester. Please see Cory or Amber if you cannot pay by tonight.

Reporter’s Report

Reporter Matt Reishman reported a new feature on our chapter’s PBL website. On the about page there is now a comment card section. Members can leave a comment or question about PBL.

Community Service Report

There was no community service committee report.

Finance Committee Report

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that the Culver’s Share Night fundraiser will take place on October 9th. Volunteers are to wear a PBL shirt, closed toed shoes, and have hair pulled back.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that the Beef O’ Brady’s fundraiser will take place on December 6th.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that we will have a Simply Swirled fundraiser. The date for this event is to be announced.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that other fundraising ideas for the semester include: pizza, bbq at towers, business sponsorships, pie a teacher, raffle to win prize.

Membership Committee Report

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will have a Halloween costume party at our committee meetings on October 29th.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported we will be having a tailgate for a SEMO Redhawks football game on November 15th or 22nd.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported we will be going to a haunted house on October 23rd or 24th.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported we will be going to Lamberts as a chapter on November 19th.

Membership committee person Emily Karleskint reported that we may go to a Blue’s game on December 8th, 11th or 16th.

Unfinished Business

There was no unfinished business.

New Business

Tyler Rosemann moved to attend the National Fall Leadership Conference in Washington D.C.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tyler Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $58.61 to Emily Rybolt for homecoming expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Announcements

Tyler Rosemann announced that all members who had not received the sign-in sheet or any new members are to report to him following the adjournment of the meeting.

Cory Herzog announced that dues of $50 for the year or $30 for the semester and $40 for the Show Me Leadership Conference are to be paid following the adjournment of the meeting.Pay Cory dues of $50 or $30.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:27 p.m.
