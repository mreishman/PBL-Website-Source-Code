Meeting Notes

SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes October 7, 2015

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, October 7, 2015, 7:35 p.m. President Cory Herzog called the meeting to order with Secretary Dylan Kennedy recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Dylan Kennedy via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported a chapter balance as of October 7, 2015, of $5,080.42. The report was filed.

Unfinished Business

None

New Business

President Cory Herzog announced Trivia Night will be Friday, October 9, 2015, at 7:00 p.m. in UC Ballroom. Members who signed up include:

  • Rebecca Ott

  • Andrew Crutcher

  • Kiana Samuel

A total of 8 members can be on a team. If you would like to participate please contact Cory by Thursday, October 08, 2015, at 5:00 p.m.

President Cory Herzog announced Field Day is coming up on October 12, 2015, at 7:00 p.m. with the Spirit Rally to take place right after at 8:00 p.m. Individuals who signed up for Field Day include:

Tug-of-War

Donut Challenge

Musical Chairs

Game of Life

  • Cassie Robertson

  • Sara Carney

  • Maria Pizzo

  • Emily Karleskint

  • Cory Herzog

  • Kyle Amuleke

  • Tyreek Williams

  • Ben Zimmer

  • Dylan Kennedy

  • Rebecca Ott

  • Misaki Makino

  • Kiana Samuel

  • Brandon Szarek

  • Gavin Ashley

  • Brandon Szarek

  • Emily Karleskint

If you cannot make it to this, you must find a replacement as soon as possible.

Individuals signed up for the Spirit Rally include:

  • Dylan Kennedy

  • Anna Richter

  • Kyle Amuleke

  • Matt Rolwing

  • Misaki Makino

President Cory Herzog reported the National Fall Leadership Conference will be November 6-7, 2015 in Omaha, Nebraska. Costs will be $180 before any fundraising. Members must contact Cory by 5:00 p.m. on Thursday, October 08, 2015, if they wish to attend.

Secretary Dylan Kennedy reported that Saturday, October 10, 2015, will be SMLC and members must state now if they would like to tour Six Flags.

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported that homecoming shirts are $12.00.

Membership chair Tyreek Williams announced a Halloween party will be coming up, cosmic bowling, and a Blues hockey game to be in the near future. No dates have been set yet.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher reported a Chick-fil-A fundraising night on Monday, October 19, 2015, from 5:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. A sign-up sheet for shifts was sent around. Money made will go towards the chapter.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher announced a Beef O’Brady’s fundraiser on Saturday, October 31, 2015, from 8:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. Tickets of $6 will be sold. Any profit made will go towards individual accounts. Please see Andrew Crutcher if you wish to sell tickets.

Fundraiser chair Andrew Crutcher reported a Wings Ect. fundraising night to be in November.

Community Service chair Melissa Stephens announced Trick-or-Treat for cans will be Wednesday, October 28, 2015. Members interested in dressing up and collecting cans need to meet in the Dempster parking lot at 6:00 p.m. Flyers will be put up around the neighborhood on Friday, October 23, 2015, at 3:00 p.m.

Community Service chair Melissa Stephens announced the March of Dimes mini walk on Saturday, November 14, 2015. The time will be announced at a later date.

Community Service chair Melissa Stephens reported highway clean up on Sunday, November 15, 2015, at 1:00 p.m.

Rebecca Ott motioned to reimburse Tyreek Williams $24.56 for tailgating expenses. Melissa Stephens seconded. Motion was passed.

Rebecca Ott motioned to reimburse Cory Herzog $46.81 for homecoming expenses. Tyreek Williams seconded. Motion was passed.

Announcements

Homecoming is in nine days, members should contact Cory Herzog, Dylan Kennedy or Tyreek Williams to work on items for the float. Members are asked to work only two hours a week on this. More information will be posted on the Facebook page, Phi Beta Lambda at Southeast.

Next chapter meeting will be Wednesday, October 24, 2015, in Dempster room 247 at 7:00 p.m.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 8:00 p.m.

Dylan Kennedy, Secretary
