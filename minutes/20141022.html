SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
October 22, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, October 22, 2014 7:02 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Tyler Rosemann via sign in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $2,028.09.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner reported that the next Show Me Day will take place on November 15th. Two volunteers are needed to stand at booth at 9 a.m. If you are interested in volunteering please speak with President Amber Joiner following the adjournment of the meeting. Volunteers are to wear a PBL shirt at this event.

President Amber Joiner reported that the March of Dimes Regional Board of Directors is looking for two students to serve on the board. Nominations for this opportunity will take place during new business.

Vice President’s Report

There was no Vice President’s Report.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that the rest of the balance for NFLC fees is due on October 19th. The total payment comes to $200.87, however $60 of this has already been paid so the amount due on the 19th is $140.87. Talk to Treasurer Cory Herzog or President Amber Joiner if you cannot make this payment by this time.

Reporter’s Report

There was no Reporter’s Report.

Membership Committee Report

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that the chapter will be oing to haunted houses this Friday. Members are to meet at 7 p.m. in the Dempster parking lot.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will have a Halloween party on October 29th after committee meetings

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will be going to Lamberts on November 19th at 4 p.m.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will be Tailgating at a football game on November 15th at 11 a.m.

Finance Committee Report

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that we will be discussing the beef stick fundraiser during unfinished business. We will be discussing which of the two companies to order from.

Community Service Committee Report

Community Service committee chairperson Tim Rosemann reported that the Trick or Treat for cans event will take place next week. Flyers will be distributed next Wednesday at 5:30 p.m. prior to the chapter meeting. Trick or Treating for cans will occur on October 31st at 6 p.m. Please don’t forget to wear your costumes.

Community Service committee chairperson Tim Rosemann reported that the Highway Cleanup will occur on October 25th. Volunteers are to meet in the Dempster parking lot at 8 a.m.

Unfinished Business

Justin Bader moved to take from the table the motion to order two boxes of beef sticks for the beef stick fundraiser. The motion was seconded and taken from the table.

The motion is to order 2 boxes of beef sticks for the beef stick fundraiser.

It was decided that we would order the beef sticks from Stoney’s.

The motion was approved.

New Business

Justin Bader moved to allocate funds of $31.47 to Dylan Kennedy for reimbursement of homecoming expenses.

Justin Bader moved to allocate funds of $31.12 to Melissa Stephens for reimbursement of homecoming expenses.

Justin Bader moved to allocate funds of $15.50 to Andrew Crutcher for reimbursement of travel expenses for the Show-Me Conference.

Justin Bader moved to allocate funds of $10 to Tyler Rosemann for reimbursement of travel expenses for the Show-Me Conference.

Justin Bader moved to allocate funds of $33.50 to Joseph Allison for reimbursement of travel expenses for the Show-Me Conference.

Justin Bader moved to open the floor for nominations for two members to be on the Regional Board of Directors for the March of Dimes.

Tim Rosemann nominated Tyler Rosemann who accepted.

Tyreek Williams nominated himself.

Emily Karleskint nominated Melissa Stephens who declined

Tyler Rosemann nominated Tim Rosemann who accepted.

Tyler Rosemann nominated Dylan Kennedy who accepted.

Tyler Rosemann moved to close the floor for nominations for the positions on the Regional Board of Directors for the March of Dimes.

Tyler Rosemann moved to vote for the positions on the Regional Board of Directors for the March of Dimes.

Tyler - TBA

Tyreek - TBA

Tim - TBA

Dylan – TBA

Dylan Kennedy will serve on the Regional Board of Directors for the March of Dimes, however there was a tie for the second candidate, therefore another vote will take place.

Tyler – TBA

Tyreek – TBA

Tim – TBA

Dylan Kennedy and Tyreek Williams will serve on the Regional Board of Directors for the March of Dimes.

Announcements

Amber Joiner announced that the next Chapter Meeting will occur on November 3rd.

Amber Joiner announced that committee meetings will occur on October 29th.

Tyler announced that if you attended Show-Me Conference please pick up t-shirt from him post adjournment.

Amber Joiner announced that if you ordered homecoming t-shirt, you are to pick it up post adjournment.

Amber Joiner announced that all members attending NFLC are to stay after the meeting.

Tyler Rosemann announced that if anyone would like a free 3x t-shirt from Show-Me Conference to please report to him post adjournment.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:26 p.m.
