SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
December 3, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, December 3, 2014 7:05 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Tyler Rosemann via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $2,386.94.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner reported that FBLA District grading will take place on January 20th for those who are interested.

President Amber Joiner reported that the SAI Forman Invitation will take place on December 5th at 6 p.m. at Bella Italia. Tickets are $20.00 for ticket with food and tickets without food is $10. The theme for this event is candy land. Attire is semi-formal.

President Amber Joiner presented information regarding the Career and Membership Achievement Program (CMAP). If you complete Director recognized at chapter. If you complete the Executive level recognized at state. If you complete president level you will be recognized at NLC and will get to attend.

Vice President’s Report

There was no Vice President’s Report.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

There was no Treasurer’s Report.

Reporter’s Report

There was no Reporter’s Report.

Membership Committee Report

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that the PBL Christmas party will take place post adjournment of the meeting.

Finance Committee Report

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that the Beef O’Brady’s fundraiser will take place on December 6th from 8 to 10 a.m.

Treasurer Cory Herzog reported that the Simply Swirled fundraiser will take place on December 15th. The time for this fundraiser is TBA.

Community Service Committee Report

Bell Ringing December 5th and 6th from 5-8

Unfinished Business

There was no unfinished business.

New Business

There was no new business.

Announcements

Amber Joiner announced that the membership induction will be next week on December 10, 2014

Amber Joiner announced that more information about the state conference competitive events will be discussed post adjournment.

Adjournment

here being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:27 p.m.

Tyler Rosemann, Secretary
