Meeting Notes

SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes of November 18, 2015

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, November 18, 2015, at 7:31 p.m. President Cory Herzog called the meeting to order with Secretary Dylan Kennedy recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Dylan Kennedy via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported a chapter balance as of November 18, 2015, of $1,571.07. The report was filed.

Unfinished Business

None

New Business

President Cory Herzog presented a recap of NFLC. Members in attendance must submit their summary to his email at cjherzog1s@semo.edu.

President Cory Herzog reported a FBLA district grading day on either January 20, 21 or 25, 2016. Ten members are required for this to happen. A sign-up sheet was passed around for members. Members that signed up include Tyreek Williams (maybe), Katie Bunselmeyer (maybe), Dylan Ashley (maybe), Cass Wiseman (maybe), Brandon Szarek, Andrew Crutcher (most likely), Benjamin Zimmer (maybe), Maria Pizzo, Melissa Stephens, Cassie Robertson, Sara Carney, Kyle Amelunke, Matt Reishman (maybe). Rebecca Ott, Cass Wiseman, Dylan Kennedy and Cory Herzog.

Secretary Dylan Kennedy reported that the homecoming pomped hippos were sold to Risco FBLA for $35. The high school chapter used them in their Halloween parade.

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported members need to see her for gas reimbursements

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported that homecoming shirts are $12 and to see her for a membership t-shirt if members have paid their dues.

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported that next semester we will be using a Square for members to swipe from their accounts.

Reporter Matt Reishman reported a new change on the website of adding galleries from the different years of PBL.

Reporter Matt Reishman presented the NFLC video he created and informed members to subscribe to the SEMO PBL YouTube channel.

Membership committee chair Tyreek Williams reported a Blues Game on December 1, 2015. Members are to sign-up or tell Tyreek immediately so he can order tickets. Members that signed up include Rebecca Ott, Kyle Amelunke, Alec Sebastian, Cass Wiseman, Brandon Szarek, and Cory Herzog.

Membership committee Tyreek Williams reported a Christmas dinner at El Sol on December 9, 2015, at 6:30 p.m. A game of Dirty Santa will be played. Members need to bring a $5 gift if they wish to play. Members that signed up include Rebecca Ott, Melissa Stephens, Cassie Robertson, Kristi Brucker, Caleb Birk, Kyle Amelunke, Sara Carney (maybe), Katie Bunselmeyer (maybe), Andrew Crutcher (maybe), Mallory Westerman (maybe), Cass Wiseman, Brandon Szarek, Gavin Ashley, Dylan Kennedy and Cory Herzog.

Membership committee Tyreek Williams discussed the membership policy. Members are allowed 2 meetings per semester to miss a meeting unexplained. If members miss more than 2 meetings, they must contact Tyreek. Members can attend different events that PBL hosts in order to dismiss their absences.

Fundraising chair Andrew Crutcher reported that the fundraising committee is working a trivia night, an FBLA workshop day, and share nights at Culvers and Burritoville for next semester.

Community service chair Melissa Stephens reported Feed My Starving Children on December 5, 2015. Please contact Melissa ASAP if interested.

Community service chair Melissa Stephens reported that she will print off flyers for the business department toy drive for members to pass out. Please see Melissa for more details.

Rebecca Ott moved to reimburse Tyreek Williams $84.71 for gas to NFLC. Melissa Stephens seconded. Motion passed.

Rebecca Ott moved to reimburse Kyle Amelunke $93.74 for gas for NFLC. Melissa Stephens seconded. Motion passed.

Announcements

Next chapter meeting will be Wednesday, December 2, 2015.

Member induction will be the next meeting. Members must be in professional dress.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:58 p.m.

Dylan Kennedy, Secretary
