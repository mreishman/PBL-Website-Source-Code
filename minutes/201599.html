Meeting Notes

SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes September 9, 2015

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, September 9, 2015, 7:05 p.m. President Cory Herzog called the meeting to order with Secretary Dylan Kennedy recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Dylan Kennedy via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Rebecca Ott reported a chapter balance as of September 9, 2015, of $3,516.65. The report was filed.

Unfinished Business

None

New Business

President Cory Herzog announced dues of $50/year or $30/semester. A date has not yet been set, but members will be given plenty of time.

Secretary Dylan Kennedy announced the Show-Me Leadership Conference at Six Flags will be on Saturday, October 10, 2015. Members interested must pay their registration of $49, as well as their dues by Wednesday, September 23, 2015.

Finance chair Andrew Crutcher announced a car wash for fundraising on September 19, 2015, from 11:00 AM to 3:00 PM at Advance Auto Parts located at 275 North Kingshighway.

Membership chair Tyreek Williams announced a BBQ at Capaha Park on Saturday, September 26, 2015. This was then changed to a tailgate before the football game outside of Houck Stadium. More details will come at a later date.

President Cory Herzog announced the homecoming festivities. Sign-up for the different activities went around the room. PBL will be participating in the games of Hungry Hungry Hippos at 6:00 PM and Battleship at 7:00 PM on Tuesday, September 15, 2015, held at the North Recreation Center. Members that signed up for Hungry Hungry Hippos include Brandon Szarek, Cass Wiseman, Oscar Sarasty, Emily Karleskint and Tyreek Williams (back up). Members that signed up for Battleship include Gavin Ashley, Dylan Ashley, Brandon Szarek, Emily Karleskint and Tyreek Williams (back up).

Announcements

A social with different kinds of board games will be held on Wednesday, September 16, 2015, at 7:00 PM in Dempster room 247.

The next chapter meeting will be Wednesday, September 23, 2015, at 7:00 PM in Demptser room 247.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:23 p.m. and members broke off into committee meetings.

Dylan Kennedy, Secretary
