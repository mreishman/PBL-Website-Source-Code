SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
October 8, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, October 8, 2014 7:04 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Tyler Rosemann via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $4,791.41.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner reported that the Show Me Leadership Conference will take place on October 18. If you are driving, fill up your car with gas before you leave then fill up your car with gas once you get home. Use the second receipt as the receipt you will give to the officers for reimbursement.

President Amber Joiner reported that those attending NFLC are to stay post adjournment of the meeting for further details on the conference.

Vice President’s Report

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that we won 1st place in float and our division of Homecoming.

Vice President Emily Rybolt stated that all Homecoming t-shirts ordered are to be picked up proceeding adjournment.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

There was no Treasurer’s Report.

Reporter’s Report

There was no Reporter’s Report.

Membership Committee Report

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that the planned Blues game will take place next semester.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that there will be a Halloween party on October 29. Look online for a survey on movies to watch.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that there will be a Haunted House event on October 24 at either 6 or 7 p.m.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will be going to Lamberts on November 19th at 4 p.m.

Membership committee chairperson Emily Karleskint reported that we will be Tailgating at a football game on November 15th at 11 a.m.

Finance Committee Report

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that Culver’s Night is tomorrow night from 5-9 p.m. Volunteers are still needed so if you would like to volunteer please contact Joseph.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that we will be doing a beef stick fundraiser this semester.

Community Service Report

Community Service committee chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that the Trick or Treat for Cans will take place on Wednesday, October 29th 5:30 p.m. (distribution of flyers) and Friday, October 31st (collection of cans). Friday’s time is to be announced.

Community Service committee chairperson Bijan Tahghighi reported that the Highway cleanup will take place on October 25th at 8 a.m.

Unfinished Business

There was no unfinished business.

New Business

Bijan Tahghighi resigned as the chairperson for the community service committee.

The resignation was approved by the chapter.

Tyler Rosemann moved to open the floor for a special election for the position of Community Service committee chairperson.

Tyler Rosemann nominates Tim Rosemann who accepted.

Harman Malhi nominated herself.

Tyler Rosemann moved to close the floor for nominations for the office of Community Service committee chairperson.

Tyler Rosemann moved to vote for the office of Community Service committee chairperson.

Tim- 9

Harman- 5

Tim Rosemann will be the Community Service committee chairperson for the remainder of the 2014-2015 academic school year.

Joseph Allison moved to order two beef stick boxes for our beef stick fundraiser.

Seconded.

Cory Herzog moved to amend the motion by striking out two and inserting one.

The motion was tabled until the next chapter meeting. Further discussion on this matter will take place in unfinished business in our next chapter meeting.

Announcements

Next chapter October 22nd.

We will not have committee meetings next week due to Fall Break.

If you are attending NFLC please stay after this meeting!

Tyler announced the Career Services sponsored Career Fair tomorrow from 10-2 at North Rec.

Matt Reishman shared a video from Homecoming.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:34 p.m.
