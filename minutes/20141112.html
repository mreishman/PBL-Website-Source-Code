SOUTHEAST MISSOURI STATE UNIVERSITY

PHI BETA LAMBDA

Minutes (Chapter Meeting)
November 12, 2014

Call to Order

The chapter meeting of Phi Beta Lambda, Zeta Alpha Lambda Chapter was called to order on Wednesday, November 12, 2014 7:07 p.m. President Amber Joiner called the meeting to order with Secretary Tyler Rosemann recording the minutes.

Attendance

Roll was taken by Secretary Tyler Rosemann via sign-in sheet.

Treasurer’s Report

Treasurer Corey Herzog reported that the chapter currently has funds of $1,540.80.

President’s Report

President Amber Joiner reported that the Scott Central FBLA March of Dimes Mini Walk will occur on Saturday, November 15th. Members who wish to attend should meet in the Dempster parking lot at 9:00 a.m. Please let Amber know if you would like to attend.

President Amber Joiner reported that chapter induction will occur on December 3rd. Members are expected to dress in business professional attire for this meeting.

President Amber Joiner reported that state competition information will be distributed in the first couple of meetings in December.

Vice President’s Report

Vice President Emily Rybolt reported that if anyone knows of someone or would like to see someone specifically who you would like to see as a speaker please let her know.

Secretary’s Report

There was no Secretary’s Report.

Treasurer’s Report

There was no Treasurer’s Report.

Reporter’s Report

There was no Reporter’s Report.

Membership Committee Report

Secretary Tyler Rosemann reported that the chapter will be going to Lamberts on November 19th. Members interested in going should meet in the Dempster parking lot at 4:00 p.m.

Secretary Tyler Rosemann reported that the chapter will be tailgating at the Southeast football game on November 22nd at 11:00 a.m. near the South Rec.

Finance Committee Report

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that the chapter will be participating in a Simply Swirled fundraiser night on either December 8th, 15th, or 29th.

Finance committee chairperson Joseph Allison reported that if anyone would like to sell beef sticks, stop by Ortiz office to pick them up.

Community Service Committee Report

Community Service committee chairperson Tim Rosemann reported that the chapter will be participating in The Salvation Army bell ringing on December 4th and 5th from 3:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at Walmart.

Unfinished Business

There was no unfinished business.

New Business

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $178.01 to Amber Joiner for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $12 to Rebecca Ott for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $12 to Tyreek Williams for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $12 to Tyler Rosemann for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $31.58 to Emily Rybolt for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tim Rosemann moved to allocate funds of $36 to Cory Herzog for the reimbursement of NFLC expenses.

The motion was seconded and approved.

Tyreek Williams moved to have the Simply Swirled fundraiser on the date of December 15th.

Tyler moved to amend the motion by striking out the word 15th and replacing it with 8th.

The motion was seconded and approved.

The fundraiser will take place on December 8th.

Announcements

Amber Joiner announced that the next chapter meeting/induction will occur on December 3rd.

Amber Joiner announced that the Harrison College of Business is hosting a toy drive.

Amber Joiner announced that the Harrison College of Business will be decorating its Christmas tree on Tuesday, November 25th at 5:00 p.m.

Adjournment

There being no further business, the meeting was adjourned at 7:19 p.m.

Tyler Rosemann, Secretary
